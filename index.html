About Me

I'm an independent game developer based in Belarus, working under the creative label Amidemon 4 Games. My focus is on magical, cozy, and educational experiences that blend visual storytelling with playful mechanics.

Current Project: Alchematrix

Alchematrix is a mobile puzzle game where players mix magical ingredients, solve math-based challenges, and unlock alchemical secrets. Designed for both fun and learning, it features modular difficulty, visual effects, and a rewarding progression system.
